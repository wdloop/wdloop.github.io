About Marawana

Marawana is a distinguished international fashion model known for her unique presence on the runway and exceptional versatility in front of the camera. With a career spanning major fashion capitals including Paris, New York, Milan, Dubai, London, and Tokyo, she has become a sought-after name in the industry.

Born in Morocco and raised with a multicultural perspective, Marawana brings a distinctive blend of cultural influences to her work, making her a favorite among avant-garde designers and established fashion houses alike.

Her portfolio includes collaborations with renowned photographers, features in leading fashion magazines, and appearances in high-profile campaigns for luxury brands worldwide.

About Me

Currently a Model & Student that's studying to be an accountant at Utah Tech University. Since my childhood I've always been passionate about clothes and fashion. An interest that was influenced by mom being a seamstress and I would watch her sew beautiful pieces for her clients.

I have always believed that what I wear can have an impact on my mood and I am hoping to transition my portfolio to modest modeling which will lead me into becoming a full-time Muslim model. My mission is to represent the Muslim community through fashion and modeling.
